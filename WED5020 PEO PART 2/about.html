Our Story

HealthCare+ was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone in our community. What started as a small family practice has grown into a comprehensive healthcare facility serving thousands of patients across Nelspruit and the surrounding areas.

Our journey began when Dr. Sarah Johnson, a passionate family physician, recognized the need for a more patient-centered approach to healthcare. She envisioned a facility where patients wouldn't just receive medical treatment, but would feel truly cared for and supported throughout their health journey.

Today, HealthCare+ stands as a testament to that vision. We've assembled a team of highly qualified medical professionals who share our commitment to excellence and patient care. Our facility has grown to include multiple specialties, state-of-the-art equipment, and a warm, welcoming environment that puts patients at ease.
